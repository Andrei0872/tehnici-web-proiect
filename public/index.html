FAQ

How can I add my own law firm on this platform?

In order to to this, you'll need to have a law firm account on this platform. This can be done by clicking on the Login/Register button and selecting the right options.

How can I add myself as a lawyer on this platform?

Once you registered as a lawyer, you're automatically added in the lawyer list. You can disabled this behavior when you're creating your account and choose when to be visible or not from your dashboard

You can create an account by clicking on the Login/Register button and selecting the right options.

If I only want to hire a lawyer, do I need to have an account?

The answer is yes. This is because we want to offer the best experience when collaborating with lawyers and in order to do so, we'll need you to have an account. Once this is done, you can bookmark lawyers and law firms, chat with lawyers right on the platform and much more.
